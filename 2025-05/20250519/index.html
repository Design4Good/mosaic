MOSAICC project - Mosaic 1: United in Pride

Usage Guide

  1. Viewing Image Tiles
    To explore an image tile in more detail, right-click on it. This action will open a pop-up featuring an enlarged version of the image along with additional context.
  2. Accessing Instagram Content
    Some tiles incorporate content from Instagram. To view these, ensure you're logged into your Instagram account. If you don't have one, you can create a free account at instagram.com.
  3. Refreshing the View
    If the mosaic appears misaligned or disoriented, refreshing the page should restore the intended layout.
